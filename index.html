07 — FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Q. Is Formaum a dedicated GoHighLevel migration specialist?
Yes. This is the only thing I do. Every engagement is a bespoke, end-to-end GoHighLevel migration from an existing CRM or fragmented tool stack into a production-grade GHL architecture. Not automation consulting. Not general operations work. GHL migration, specifically.
Q. What's included in an end-to-end migration?
Full audit of current tools and data. Bespoke pipeline architecture designed for your business, not a template. Custom fields, stages, and exit paths. Workflow builds. Data migration. Integrations with your existing systems. Staff training. Pilot launch at one location. Sequential rollout to remaining locations. Enterprise production-grade from day one.
Q. What CRMs do you migrate from?
Monday.com, HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Airtable, and any fragmented stack of disconnected tools. Every migration is bespoke to the source system and target architecture. No two builds are the same.
Q. Do you handle multi-location franchise rollouts?
Yes. Multi-location rollouts are a core specialty. Each location gets its own GHL sub-account with localized branding, pipelines, and staff access. Rollouts follow a pilot-first methodology: prove the architecture at one location, then deploy to the rest sequentially. Same architecture, location-specific execution.
Q. What makes this enterprise production-grade?
I was a production manager at EA Sports FIFA — $100M productions, 200+ people, a $7B franchise. Every migration I run is managed like a real production: structured phases, clear deliverables, weekly checkpoints, and no hand-waving. Enterprise discipline applied to CRM migration. That's the difference.
Q. How long does a migration take?
Single-location builds: 3-4 weeks. Multi-location franchise rollouts: 6-8 weeks for the pilot, then sequential deployment to remaining locations. Every engagement has a clear timeline defined upfront — no open-ended scope, no surprises.
