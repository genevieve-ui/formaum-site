08 — FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Q. Can't I just map this myself?
Sure, but you'll spend 10+ hours researching platforms and still won't know which gaps to fix first. Most businesses have blind spots they can't see from the inside.
Q. What if I've already tried mapping my journey?
Great. I'll look at what you've mapped, identify where your assumptions are off, and show you the gaps your team can't see.
Q. Do I need to prepare anything?
Not really. Our first call is a conversation, not a presentation. I'll handle the rest.
Q. Can I bring team members?
Absolutely. The more perspectives, the richer the mapping. Bring anyone who interacts with customers.
Q. Will you recommend tools?
I stay on top of every new tool, every AI release, every platform shift - so you don't have to. If something is genuinely better than what you're using, I'll tell you. But I'm not here to overcomplicate your stack. The goal is a lean, fully functioning system - not more software to manage.
Q. What does AI search have to do with my operations?
The way leads enter your pipeline changed. Customers ask ChatGPT and Perplexity instead of scrolling Google. If your business isn't structured to show up in those answers, your funnel starves no matter how good your conversion is. I build the infrastructure that makes AI recommend you.
